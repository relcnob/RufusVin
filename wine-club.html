Wine Club

Here at RufusVin, we want our customers to enjoy wines from all the small European wineries we work with. And that is why we created the Wine Club, enabling our customers to enjoy 4 staff-selected wines which go nicely with the seasonal foods and weather.

Each month the wines included in the Wine Club membership are showcasing the prodcuts of the small wineries, sometimes never heard of, but full with passion and aroma.

The concept is simple. You can join the Wine Club as a member for a monthly fee, or you can make a one time purchase of the curated Wine Box. Give it a chance, you never know what you're missing on.
